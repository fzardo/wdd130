History

Rafting Momentum was founded in 2005 with the goal of providing exceptional white-water rafting experiences. Initially starting with a single raft and a small team of experienced guides, the company quickly gained a reputation for safety and adventure. Over the years, Rafting Momentum expanded its fleet and services, incorporating advanced equipment and diverse river routes. Today, it is recognized as a leading rafting company, known for its commitment to customer satisfaction and environmental stewardship.
